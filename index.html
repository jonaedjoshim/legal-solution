How do I know if I need a lawyer?

If you're facing legal issues like disputes, criminal charges, divorce, or need legal documents reviewed, consulting a lawyer ensures your rights are protected and the process is legally sound.

What is the process of filing for divorce?

The divorce process typically involves filing a petition, serving your spouse, responding to the petition, negotiating terms (like child custody or property), and getting court approval.

Can I get legal aid or free consultation?

Many firms offer a free initial consultation to understand your case. Legal aid may be available depending on your income level and case type.

How long does a criminal case usually take?

It varies by complexity, evidence, and court schedules. A simple case may resolve in a few weeks, while complex cases can take months or even years.
